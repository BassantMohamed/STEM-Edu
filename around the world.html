STEM Around the world

STEM is an educational program that is applied all around the world


Massachusetts Institute of Technology

MIT's strength lie in the fields of engineering, science and technology, and its exclusivity involving which students they accept means that only the very best make the cut.



United States naval academy

The USNA STEM Center is focused on addressing an urgent national need for more young people to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering and mathematics.



STEM schools in Egypt

The Egyptian STEM schools are high schools of learning launched to provide youth with world-class education in the critical areas of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.



Cornell university

New Cornell tech campus on Roosevelt Island will create opportunities for all people by adding much-needed STEM emphasis to education systems.



Rice University

The Rice University supports and promotes wide ranging efforts to improve science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) education in Houston-area school districts.
